About Gorillas

Gorillas are large aggressive mammals. The males are called Sivlerbacks, because of the silver fur on their back's. They love to eat people and watch The Office.

Gorillas are found in part of the eastern Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), Uganda and Rwanda, while mountain gorillas are only found within the Virunga mountain region straddling the DRC, Rwanda and Uganda borders, as well as the Bwindi Impenetrable National Park in Uganda. They can get up to 5'6" and weigh around 350lbs.
